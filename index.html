Our Services

Correctional Services

We include all our inmates in a correctional program that seeks to ensure they are transformed when they get back to society.

Counselling

Inmates with psychological issues undergo thorough counselling to relieve them of their troubles and turn them into sane and stable men.

Economic Empowerment

All our inmates go through programs that give them various enterpreneural and business skills, and ensure they have a skill to depend on once they get back to society.
